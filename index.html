Questions

Frequently Asked

Everything you need to know before your first ride in Boston.

How does daily billing work? +
You're charged once per day for each day you keep the scooter. There are no long-term contracts — simply return the scooter to any Boston hub whenever you're done, and billing stops automatically.
Do I need a license to ride in Boston? +
You need to be at least 18 with a valid ID. No motorcycle license is required for our electric scooters under Massachusetts law. We'll brief you on Boston's scooter rules at pickup.
What if the scooter breaks down? +
Contact our 24/7 Boston support line. We'll either guide you through a quick fix or dispatch a replacement scooter to your location within the hour. You won't be charged for downtime.
Is there a discount for long-term rentals? +
Yes! Rentals of 7+ days get 10% off, and 30+ days get 20% off the daily rate. Discounts are applied automatically at checkout.
Can I ride to Cambridge and Somerville? +
Absolutely! Our service area covers Greater Boston including Cambridge, Somerville, Brookline, and more. You can pick up and return at our Cambridge hub too.
