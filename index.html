Questions

Frequently Asked

Everything you need to know before your first ride.

How does daily billing work? +
You're charged once per day for each day you keep the scooter. There are no long-term contracts — simply return the scooter whenever you're done, and billing stops automatically.
Do I need a license to ride? +
Requirements depend on your local regulations. In most cities, you need to be at least 18 with a valid ID. No motorcycle license is required for our standard scooters.
What if the scooter breaks down? +
Contact our 24/7 support line. We'll either guide you through a quick fix or dispatch a replacement scooter to your location within the hour. You won't be charged for downtime.
Is there a discount for long-term rentals? +
Yes! Rentals of 7+ days get 10% off, and 30+ days get 20% off the daily rate. Discounts are applied automatically at checkout.
Where can I pick up and return scooters? +
We have multiple hubs across the city. You can pick up from any location and return to any location — they don't need to be the same one.
